ABOUT US

All INFORMATION IN THIS WEBSITE IS TAKEN FROM WHO & AVERT.

WHO (World Health Organization)

Founded: 7 April 1948
Headquarters: Geneva, Switzerland
Vision & Mission: The attainment by all people of the highest possible level of health. WHO leads global efforts to expand universal health coverage and to direct international health within the United Nations' system.

AVERT

Founded: 1986
Founder: Peter Kanabus and Annabel Kanabus.
Avert is based in Brighton, United Kingdom.
The charity provides information and education on HIV/AIDS and Coronavirus worldwide.

About This Website

This website has been created to spread awareness about COVID-19, its symptoms, prevention, and treatment. The content is sourced from trusted health organizations — WHO and AVERT — to ensure accurate and reliable information.

Our goal is to educate people about:
  • What COVID-19 is and how it spreads
  • Symptoms and diagnosis
  • Prevention and safety measures
  • Treatment options available
  • Latest news and updates
